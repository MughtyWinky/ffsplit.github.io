FFSplit is a freeware program that allows you to capture or record your desktop in different high quality video formats.

Capture and Record

You can capture and record what is happening on your desktop. Stream it live or save it to your desktop to upload and share on your favorite websites.

There are plenty of features such as hotkeys, webcam and overlay for you to use to make your streams and videos more professional and unique.
